# Practice Direction 31 – Mediation / 實務指示 31 – 調解

## Part A / A 部

### 1. Underlying Objective / 基本目標
An underlying objective of the Rules of the High Court and the District Court is to facilitate the settlement of disputes. The Court has the duty as part of active case management to further that objective by encouraging the parties to use an alternative dispute resolution procedure ("ADR"). The parties and their legal representatives have the duty of assisting the Court.
《高等法院規則》和《區域法院規則》的基本目標之一是利便法律程序中的各方就其爭議達成和解。法院推行積極的案件管理，各方及其法律代表有責任協助法院。

### 4 & 5. Adverse Costs Order / 不利訟費令與合理解釋
In exercising its discretion on costs, the Court takes into account any unreasonable failure of a party to engage in mediation. 
法庭行使酌情權裁定訟費時，會考慮訴訟人沒有合理解釋但不曾參與調解一事。

**Exemptions from Adverse Costs Order / 免受訟費懲罰的情況：**
(1) The party has engaged in mediation to the minimum level of participation. (已達到最低參與程度)
(2) The party has a reasonable explanation for not engaging in mediation (e.g., active without prejudice settlement negotiations are progressing, or actively engaged in another form of ADR). (有合理原因解釋爲何不曾參與調解，例如正進行無損權利的和解談判並取得進展)

### 6. Privilege & Confidentiality / 特權與保密
What happens during the mediation process, being without prejudice communications, is protected by privilege. The Court cannot compel disclosure of or admit protected materials as evidence.
調解過程中發生的事情，屬於無損權利的通訊，受保密權保護。法庭不可強迫披露或接納此等資料為證據。

## Part B / B 部 (Legally Represented Parties / 有律師代表的程序)

### 9. Mediation Certificate / 調解證明書
Solicitors acting respectively for the parties shall file in Court a Mediation Certificate at the same time as the time tabling questionnaire under Order 25, rule 1.
代表各方行事的律師把「設定時間表的問卷」送交法院存檔的同時，必須把「調解證明書」存檔。

### 10 & 11. Mediation Notice and Response / 調解通知書與回覆書
*   **Mediation Notice (調解通知書):** Served by the Applicant as soon as practicable after filing the Mediation Certificate. (申請人存檔證明書後盡快送達另一方)
*   **Mediation Response (調解回覆書):** Respondent should respond within 14 days upon receiving the Mediation Notice. (答辯人收到通知書後 14 天內回覆)

### 13. Court Directions / 法庭指示
Where parties are unable to reach agreement on mediation proposals, they may apply to Court for directions. However, the Court cannot compel a party to engage in mediation or appoint a mediator over opposition unless both parties agree.
若各方未能就調解建議達成共識，可向法庭申請指示。惟法庭不可強迫反對的一方參與調解或強行委任調解員。

### 16. Stay of Proceedings / 擱置法律程序
The Court may stay the proceedings or any part thereof for the purpose of mediation, while avoiding disruption to trial dates.
法庭可把法律程序擱置以便各方進行調解，但盡量避免延遲審訊日期。
