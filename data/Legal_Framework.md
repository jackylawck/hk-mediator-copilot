# 香港調解立法框架 / Legislative Framework for Mediation in Hong Kong

## 1. 《調解條例》(第620章) / Mediation Ordinance (Cap. 620)
*   **立法背景與生效日期 (Background & Commencement):** 
    Enacted in June 2012 and came into operation on 1 January 2013 to implement recommendations of the Working Group on Mediation chaired by the Secretary for Justice.
    （2012年6月制定，2013年1月1日實施，落實由律政司司長領導的調解工作小組建議。）
*   **宗旨 (Objects - Section 3):** 
    To promote, encourage and facilitate the resolution of disputes by mediation, and to protect the confidential nature of mediation communications.
    （提倡、鼓勵和促進以調解方式解決爭議，及使調解通訊得以保密。）
*   **調解定義 (Meaning of Mediation - Section 4):** 
    A structured process comprising one or more sessions in which one or more impartial individuals, without adjudicating a dispute or any aspect of it, assist the parties to a dispute to identify issues, explore/generate options, communicate, and reach agreement.
    （由不偏不倚的人喺不作裁決情況下，協助各方找出爭議點、探求解決方案、互相溝通並達成協議的有組織程序。）

## 2. 《道歉條例》(第631章) / Apology Ordinance (Cap. 631)
*   **立法背景與生效日期 (Background & Commencement):** 
    Enacted in July 2017 and came into operation on 1 December 2017 following public consultations.
    （經兩輪公眾諮詢後，於2017年7月制定，2017年12月1日生效。）
*   **目的 (Objective):** 
    To promote and encourage the making of apologies with a view to preventing the escalation of disputes and facilitating their amicable resolution.
    （提倡和鼓勵作出道歉，以期防止爭端惡化，和促進和睦排解爭端。）
*   **道歉的定義 (Meaning of Apology - Section 4):** 
    An expression of regret, sympathy or benevolence (oral, written or by conduct), including any part that is an express or implied admission of fault or liability, or a statement of fact.
    （指就某事宜表達歉意、遺憾、同情或善意，包括承認過失或法律責任的表達，或相關事實陳述。）
*   **法律效果與特權 (Legal Effects & Protection - Sections 7 & 8):** 
    *   In most civil proceedings, an apology **does NOT constitute** an express or implied admission of fault or liability (Section 7).
        （喺大部分民事法律程序中，道歉**不構成**明示或默示承認過失或法律責任。）
    *   An apology **must NOT be taken into account** in determining fault/liability to the prejudice of the apology maker (Section 7).
        （裁斷過失或法律責任時，道歉**不得列為不利於**道歉者的考慮因素。）
    *   An apology is generally **NOT admissible as evidence** for determining fault/liability to the prejudice of the apology maker (Section 8).
        （道歉的證據，一般**不得接納**為不利於道歉者的證據。）

## 3. 第三者資助調解 / Third Party Funding of Mediation
*   **《2017年仲裁及調解法例 (第三者資助)(修訂)條例》 / Amendment Ordinance 2017:** 
    Enacted on 14 June 2017 to clarify that third party funding of arbitration, mediation and related proceedings is permitted under Hong Kong law.
    （2017年6月14日通過，釐清香港法律准許第三者資助仲裁、調解及相關法律程序。）
*   **實施狀態 (Commencement Status):** 
    New provisions relating to Third Party Funding of Mediation under Cap. 620 have been deferred to a future date pending further consultation with the mediation community and relevant stakeholders.
    （關乎《調解條例》的新訂條文實施日期押後，待律政司進一步諮詢業界及持份者後決定。）
