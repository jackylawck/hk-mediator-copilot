# Practice Direction 31.1 – Case Settlement Initiatives in Civil Cases in the District Court / 實務指示 31.1 – 區域法院民事案件和解措施

## Part A & B / 引言與案件和解會議 (CSC)

### 1 & 2. Scope & Purpose / 適用範圍與目的
Applies to civil cases in the District Court (except personal injuries, employees' compensation, false detention, and equal opportunities cases). Adjudication is not the only means of resolving disputes. Unreasonable failure to engage in ADR is a ground for costs sanction.
適用於區域法院民事案件（人身傷亡、僱員補償、非法拘禁及平等機會案件除外）。另類排解爭議（ADR）係重要和解途徑，不合理不參與另類排解程序可被施加訟費懲罰。

### 8. CSC Presided by Masters / 聆案官主持 CSC
CSCs are generally listed before a master with experience in mediation to facilitate constructive dialogue, explore options, and narrow down issues.
案件和解會議一般排期由富有調解經驗嘅聆案官進行，協助訴訟各方開展有建設性對話及收窄爭論事項。

## Part D / 調解員輔助案件和解會議 (MCSC)

### 12 & 13. MCSC Nature / MCSC 性質
combines a CSC with mediation facilitated by an appointed mediator. Generally held after the parties have already undergone a mediation session.
結合案件和解會議與委任調解員（Appointed Mediator）嘅調解，作為調解嘅延續進程，通常由法官主持。

### 15 & 17. Mediator's Note & Confidentiality / 調解員摘記與保密性
Requires lodgment of a Mediator's Note (common grounds, remaining issues, latest proposals). All communications between the Court and the appointed mediator are confidential and without prejudice.
須遞交調解員摘記（共識事項、爭論議題、最新建議）。區院與委任調解員之間嘅所有溝通內容（包括摘記）均屬保密及無損權利性質。

## Part F & G / 出席要求與會議程序

### 23. Mandatory Attendance / 必須出席人士
*   Lay parties (natural persons) / 自然人訴訟方
*   Authorized corporate representatives with settlement authority / 獲授權且有權和解嘅法團代表
*   Legal representatives / 法律代表
*   Appointed Mediator (for MCSC) / 委任調解員（如屬 MCSC）

### 26 & 27. Without Prejudice & Limited Jurisdiction / 無損權益與有限管治權
*   CSCs/MCSCs are conducted on a without prejudice basis; statements made are inadmissible in later proceedings. (無損權益基礎進行，所說的話不可接納為證據)
*   **Limited Jurisdiction (有限司法管轄權):** Masters/Judges do NOT adjudicate disputes. Orders are limited to:
    1. Adjourning the CSC/MCSC (押後會議)
    2. Consent orders disposing fully/partially of the case (同意令)
    3. Directions to progress the case to the next stage (推進至下一階段指示)

## Part H / 訟費規定

### 32. Costs Sanctions / 訟費懲罰
Default in compliance with directions for preparation or unreasonable conduct at the CSC/MCSC may result in costs sanctions.
不遵從準備指示或喺會議中有不合理行為，均可能受到訟費懲罰。
